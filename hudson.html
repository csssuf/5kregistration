Are You Ready!


Pre-Register today for the CSH 5K, being held October 25, 2014. Tickets are only $10.00 for a limited time! Act fast and save! All proceeds will go to support Charity:Water to help provide clean water to people all across the world!

What is it?

When

The 5K will be held Saturday, October 25, 2014 at 10:00 AM.

Where

The race will begin behind Nathaniel Rochester Hall on the residence side of campus and work its way around and through the academic side and back. Check out the map below for the exact route!

Who's it For

All of the proceeds collected from this race will go to benefit Charity:Water. Charity:Water is a non-profit organization bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing nations. They use 100% of public donations directly toward the development of clean water resources, so all of the money raised will go directly to helping those who need it most!
MORE INFORMATION

Early Registration

Early registration is required to participate in this event! Be sure to register with a valid email using our provided registration field. Once you've registered, you will be emailed a confirmation, where you can either pay via credit card, or opt to pay at the event!

Event Pricing

Tickets will be on sale starting Saturday, Sept. 27. The $10.00 Early Bird Special will be offered from Sept. 27 - Oct. 4. General ticket sales go on sale Oct. 5 - Oct. 11 for $15.00!

I've Registered! What's next?

Once you have selected your payment option, your spot will be held in our system! On race day, you will receive a runner's bib with your number at one of our check-in tables. Check-in begins at 9:00 AM, with the race starting promptly at 10:00 AM
